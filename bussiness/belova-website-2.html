Our Story

Two women, one new beginning.

Belova was founded by Gauri and Preeti — a homemaker and a teacher who, at the age of fifty, decided it was time to build something entirely their own. Alongside the lives they had already built, they turned their instinct for warmth and thoughtfulness into a business, choosing to start rather than settle into the idea that it was "too late."

What began as a shared idea between two friends soon became Belova — a gifting brand built on the belief that a gift chosen with care can carry more meaning than anything money alone can buy.

Beautiful, and mindful

We believe gifting doesn't have to cost the earth. Belova is committed to sustainable practices — from thoughtfully sourced products to packaging that's kinder to the planet, without ever compromising on elegance.
